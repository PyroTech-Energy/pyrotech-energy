    SOLVING A MAJOR PROBLEM

    ALMOST NO PLASTIC WASTE IS RECYCLED

    Every year, almost 300 million tons of plastic trash are created.
    Recycling or treating this plastic requires very expensive and complex infrastructure, which is extremely difficult to develop.

    For this reason, only 9% of plastics are recycled worldwide, leaving the other 91% to be burned or dumped in nature.

    PYROTECH is solving this problem by creating a small scale pyrolysis reactor, enabling everyone to easily turn plastics into fuel at home by themselves.
    Our product has the potential to massively reduce plastic pollution while creating a increasingly valuable ressource out of trash.
